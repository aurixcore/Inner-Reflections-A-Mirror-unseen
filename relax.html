Breath & Relaxation

Take a moment to focus on your breathing. Try this exercise:

  1. Inhale slowly for 4 counts.
  2. Hold your breath for 4 counts.
  3. Exhale slowly for 6 counts.
  4. Repeat 3–5 times.
